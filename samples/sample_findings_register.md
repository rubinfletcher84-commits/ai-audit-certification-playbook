# Sample AI Audit Findings Register

| ID | Domain | Finding | Severity | Owner | Target Date | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| F-01 | AI Inventory | Generative AI tools are being used in two business units without inclusion in the central AI register. | High | CIO / AI Program Lead | 30 days | Open |
| F-02 | Vendor Assurance | Contract with chatbot provider does not clearly restrict customer-data use for model improvement. | High | Procurement / Legal | 45 days | Open |
| F-03 | Monitoring | No formal post-launch quality and harm review exists for the internal HR assistant. | Critical | HR System Owner | 15 days | Open |
| F-04 | Governance | AI policy exists, but no evidence of quarterly governance review meetings was provided. | Medium | AI Governance Lead | 30 days | Open |
| F-05 | Human Oversight | Human review is documented for high-impact AI decisions, but escalation criteria are unclear. | Medium | Business Owner | 60 days | Open |

## Notes
This sample findings register shows how audit issues can be tracked by:
- domain
- severity
- owner
- due date
- remediation status

In a future version, this can be converted into an Excel sheet or dashboard.
