# Project Overview: AI Audit Checklist & Certification Playbook

## Project purpose
This project was built to turn high-level AI governance frameworks into a practical audit and certification-readiness toolkit that organizations can actually use.

## Problem it addresses
Many teams know they need AI governance, but they do not have a structured way to:
- inventory AI systems
- assess risk and impact
- test controls
- evaluate generative AI use
- track findings
- prepare for certification readiness

## What this project includes
- full AI audit playbook in PDF and DOCX
- governance checklist
- generative AI checklist
- vendor assurance checklist
- certification readiness checklist
- sample findings register
- sample AI inventory

## Frameworks used
- NIST AI RMF
- ISO/IEC 42001
- ISO/IEC 23894
- ISO/IEC 42005
- NIST AI RMF Generative AI Profile
- EU AI Act basics

## Why this matters
Organizations are adopting AI faster than many of them can govern it. This project helps bridge the gap between framework knowledge and field-ready audit execution.
